About carfiguration.com

Carfiguration is a hobby project built by car enthusiasts, for car enthusiasts. Our goal is a simple, no frills, no bullshit directory for car information online. We want to to make it easy for you to conduct thought experiments on your future garage and access high quality automotive related websites.

The links here target an English-speaking USA audience by default. It's worth noting that international car manufacturers such as Lotus may have information available on more models by going to the international version of their website.

Link Information

  • Indirect Indirect link. The manufacturer does not have a configurator that displays all models up front; rather users will need to find the model of the car they're interested in and then find the configurator themselves.
  • Info Only Informational only - no configurators available.
  • X Separate Some models, such as the Ford GT, have a separate configurator. The main link brings you to the configurator for most of the manufacturer's vehicles.
