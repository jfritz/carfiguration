About carfiguration.com

Carfiguration is a hobby project built by car enthusiasts, for car enthusiasts. Our goal is a simple, no frills, no bullshit directory for car information online. We want to to make it easy for you to conduct thought experiments on your future garage and access high quality automotive related websites.
